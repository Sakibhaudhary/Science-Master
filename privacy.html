Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Science Master collects, uses, and protects your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Email address, name (when provided)
  • Usage Data: Formulas used, calculations performed, favorite formulas
  • Technical Data: Device information, browser type, IP address
  • Authentication Data: Firebase authentication tokens (when logged in)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve our educational services
  • Personalize your learning experience
  • Respond to your inquiries and provide support
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve our platform

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is securely stored using:

  • Firebase: For authentication and data storage
  • Encryption: All sensitive data is encrypted
  • Secure Protocols: HTTPS for all data transmission

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase: For authentication and database services
  • Font Awesome: For icons and UI elements
  • Google Fonts: For typography

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Export your data
  • Opt-out of data collection (by not creating an account)

6. Cookies and Tracking

We use minimal cookies for:

  • Authentication sessions
  • User preferences
  • Basic analytics

You can control cookies through your browser settings.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify users of significant changes through the platform or via email.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, please contact us at:

Email: privacy@sciencemaster.com

Our Commitment

We are committed to protecting your privacy and providing a safe learning environment. Your data is used solely to enhance your educational experience and is never sold to third parties.
